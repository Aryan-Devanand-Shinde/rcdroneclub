About Us ?

Team Vajra is a interdisciplinary technical club consisting of highly motivated candidates who have passion for Aeromodelling and drones. We believe in catering the industrial and social needs by developing skilled manpower in drone technology.We aim in resolving real-time challenges by sparking interest about drone technology in young minds.
